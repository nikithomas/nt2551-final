Homework 4: This homework served as an introduction to object oriented programming, through the creation of a class with students and assignments.

Homework 5: This assignment involved the completion of the machine learning library that was worked on throughout the course of the semester.

Out of all the assignments, my favorite was the first one. Even though it was difficult to do as a beginer, I felt as though it really helped develop an understanding of the fundamental principles of python that would be used for the remainder of the course.
